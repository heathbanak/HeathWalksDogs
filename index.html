ABOUT

Welcome to Heath Walks Dogs!

a small dog walking service based out of Holliston, MA.

Hi, I'm Heath!

I am a musician, web developer, and animal lover from Holliston, MA, and I am eager and excited to care for your pet! Caring for pets has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember: in a family that loves their pets as much as their children, my life has included animal care every single day. I have a true love and appreciation for animals and how much they can improve quality of life. I understand the importance your pets hold and that, just like us, they know that companionship has no replacement.

Dog walking and house sitting has given me the opportunity to extend this passion for animal care to people all around me. Whether you have a new puppy who can't be alone for too long, a senior dog that needs extra loving, or a cat who could use some company during the day, I am truly so excited to be with them. Most importantly, I will always treat your pet as if they were my own.

I have 6 years of experience in animal care, and while I work mostly with dogs, I have experience caring for cats, reptiles, and smaller mammals like hamsters. I am comfortable and experienced in walking/running (on- or off-leash), playing, feeding, administering medication, and, of course, loving, pets. If requested, I will send pictures from my time with your pet and I always keep you updated on their health, mood, and behavior.

I also work through Rover.com and am background-checked through a third-party service, Checkr. For more information on background checks and Rover, you can visit my profile here.
