    Dog Walking

    Exercise

    • can take pets to other locations to exercise, like Ashland State Park
      • allows for interaction with other pets
    more info

      Exercise can be integral to your pets' health and happiness. If you have a very active pet, I am able to provide long walks and playtime to ensure that their exercise needs are being met. If you have a fenced yard, or your dog(s) can be off-leash, I can combine both a walk and off-leash play in one visit. If your dog interacts well with other dogs and can be off-leash, I can also take them to the Ashland State Park — there you will find a large field for all dogs to run, play, and enjoy together, along with trails through a wooded area surrounding the reservoir.

    For Less Active Pets
    • happy to service senior pets and/or less active pets
    • pet receives full-length visit regardless of activities
    • whatever your pet may need, indoors or outdoors, active or non-active, will be supplied happily
    • pet sets our boundaries
    more info

      That being said, I am aware that not every pet needs this amount of activity. I am more than happy to spend time with less active pets, including senior pets, who may benefit from a less intense visit. Many people hire a dog walker to help their pet feel less lonely while they are away or at work, and the best thing for some pets is simply company. When I am visiting a less-active pet, I ensure that all of my time remains focused on them. Whatever they like to do to feel most comfortable, whether that be getting lots of pets, snuggling up on the couch, playing with toys, or just relaxing, my job is to increase their happiness in any way I can. I understand that not all pets are snugglers, and that sometimes the best thing for a pet is simply to know that someone else is there. I will always give your pet the space they need and let THEM set our boundaries.

    All in all, my services can and will be tailored to exactly what you and your pet(s) need. As your needs change, so will my services.

    House Sitting

    House Sitting

      House sitting is typically when a sitter stays at your home with your pets while you are away for an extended period of time (at least one night). The service is best for pets who do not do well away from home, as an alternative option to a kennel or boarding your pet. As a house sitter, my job is to care for your pet in the same way you would, and to help them feel comfortable and safe while you are away.

    more info

      Put yourself in your pets' situation: The people they see everyday suddenly aren't there (maybe for the first time) and are replaced by a stranger! Many pets are scared by this and can act very nervously while their owners are away, especially if they are unfamiliar with the new person in their home. This is another reason why our meet-ups are integral — the more comfortable your pet is with me before you leave, the more successful and enjoyable our stay together will be.
      For more nervous or protective pets, I recommend multiple meet-ups or even scheduling a few walks before you leave. Your pet is much less likely to be nervous without you if they already recognize me as a caretaker.

      What I do with your pet can as much up to you or up to me as you would like. If they have a strict feeding/exercise schedule, I will be sure to follow this to a t (routines can be as important in your pets' life as they are in ours!). If not, I will be sure to keep them busy with lots of play and pets.

    All in all, my services can and will be tailored to exactly what you and your pet(s) need. As your needs change, so will my services.

    Meet Ups

    *meet-ups are required prior to any service*

      Before I accept a new client, we always meet-up ahead of time so that I can meet them and their pet(s). I understand that some pets may need more than one visit to be comfortable with a new person, and I will work with them for however long they need to establish a good relationship between us. Building a relationship with your pet is the most important things to me; I am here to help your pet be happy and comfortable, and the first step is for them to know that I am a friend.

    Meet-Ups

    Meet-ups are required prior to any service.

    Keeping your pet(s) safe and happy is my priority, and meet-ups are the best way to ensure that your pet(s) and I are a good fit. Some pets may take longer than others to feel comfortable with a new walker/sitter. Establishing a relationship between us is the first step.
